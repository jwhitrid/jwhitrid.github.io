Ethics in Information Technology

Information technology professionals and developers must adhere to ethical practices to ensure the fair and responsible use of technology. Below are key guidelines:

  • Ensure user data privacy and security.
  • Avoid creating biased algorithms that may result in discrimination.
  • Respect intellectual property rights and prevent software piracy.
  • Disclose any conflicts of interest in data analysis and reporting.
